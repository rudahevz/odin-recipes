Beef Stroganoff

Beef Stroganoff is typically a rich dish, yet it's quite simple to make. This recipe uses nonfat sour cream to help cut down on the fat content.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sirloin steak, cut into bite size strips
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 ¼ cups fat free sour cream
  • 1 (8 ounce) package egg noodles

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Brown the beef strips in a large skillet over medium high heat and drain excess fat. Add the onion and saute for 5 minutes, or until tender. Stir in the flour, salt, paprika and ground black pepper.

  2. Step 2

    3. Add the soup, reduce heat to medium low and let simmer for 15 minutes. Then add the sour cream, stirring well.

  3. Step 3

    4. Prepare egg noodles according to package directions. Serve beef mixture over the egg noodles.
